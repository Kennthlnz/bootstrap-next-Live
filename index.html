What equiptment do we need?

That can vary on the type of playback and synchronization you are trying to accomplish.
You will need at least a computer running Ableton Live 10 or newer. If you are wanting to use MIDI cues to control Pro Prester, your lighting console, Video wall, etc, that will depend on your setup.

If you have questions, please contact us.

Does Next Live Supply or Make Tracks?

Not at this time.
Because of copywrite and legal restrictions, you will need to supply us with access to your MultiTracks, LoopCommunity account, or a Tracks Library.

Is there flexibility in how the session are formatted?

Yes!
We can make any session in Arrangement or Session View. For ease of use we normally suggest Session View.
We will go over this with you in our weekly meetings.

How are Payment Made?

Payments will be processed through PayPal or StripeBank.
If you are on a subscription, payment will be drafted on the first of the month.
